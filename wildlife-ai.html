Wildlife AI Monitor

TensorFlow Lite · Google Coral · Edge TPU · NVIDIA Jetson · 2023

Developed and deployed an AI-powered bird tracking device to monitor endangered and invasive species for a local nature center. Implemented a TensorFlow Lite BirdNet model on a Google Coral Board with an Edge TPU, optimizing real-time acoustic classification in a Linux environment.

Hardware: Google Coral Dev Board (Edge TPU)

Model: BirdNet (TensorFlow Lite, acoustic bird ID)

OS: Linux

Use case: Monitoring endangered and invasive species for a local nature center
