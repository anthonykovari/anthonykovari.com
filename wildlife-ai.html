Wildlife AI Monitor

TensorFlow Lite · Google Coral · Edge TPU · NVIDIA Jetson · 2023

A solar-powered edge AI device deployed in the field to autonomously identify bird species in the wild. Built around a Google Coral Board with an Edge TPU running BirdNet, a TensorFlow Lite acoustic classification model, the device operates entirely off-grid.

Sightings are logged in real time to a citizen science bird database, contributing wildlife data to broader ecological research without any manual collection. Also ported to an NVIDIA Jetson board for classroom demonstrations.

Hardware: Google Coral Dev Board (Edge TPU), NVIDIA Jetson

Model: BirdNet (TensorFlow Lite, acoustic bird ID)

Power: Solar-powered, fully off-grid deployment

Integration: Real-time citizen science database logging
